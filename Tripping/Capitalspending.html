NORTH GOA

North Goa is a district in the state of Goa, India. It's known for its beaches, culture, and nightlife. North Goa is generally more expensive than South Goa

SOUTH GOA

South Goa is a district in the state of Goa, India. It's known for its natural beaches, temples, waterfalls, hill spots, paddy fields, and coconut groves.

PANJIM

Panjim, also known as Panaji, is the capital of Goa, India. It's located on the banks of the Mandovi River in the Tiswadi sub-district. Panjim is known for its beaches, churches, carnivals, and natural beauty.

MAPUSA

Mapusa is a town in North Goa.Mapusa is famous for its colorful market, which sells fresh local food, spices, and other items. Mapusa is also home to the Church of Our Lady of Miracles, also known as St Jerome's, which was founded in 1594
