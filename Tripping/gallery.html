Nature

Goa is a tropical paradise with a hot and humid climate year-round. It's located in the tropical zone and near the Arabian Sea. The hottest month is May, with temperatures over 35°C (95°F) and high humidity. Monsoon rains arrive in early June and provide some relief from the heat.
